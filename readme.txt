Instantly drives traffic to your site by communicating with your readers via a Chrome extension.
== Description ==

This plugin allows you to instantly attract your fans to your site by broadcasting straight to their Chrome browser.

As soon as you publish a post, your readers will automatically see a link, and your author picture in their browser.

This allows you to instantly drive traffic to your blog, as soon as you finish your blog post!

Here is the best part: the plugin automatically generates the extension for you to upload to the Chrome store - no need for coding!

== Installation ==


1. Upload the content of `wp2crx.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to be a coder to use this plugin? =

No, there is absolutely zero need for coding - the plugin automatically 
generates the extension for you. You only have to put in credentials for your 
Google account - but the links are provided for you to do that.
